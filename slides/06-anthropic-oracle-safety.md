## Slide 2: The Anthropic Discovery (Jan 2026)
**"Persona Drift"** — AI personalities shift during long conversations

Key findings:
- AI has multiple "zones" (Persona Space)
- Long chats drift away from safety
- Emotional topics = higher risk
- Technical guardrails help (50% safer)

---

## Slide 3: What is Persona Space?
Think of AI as having a "activity ground" with many corners:
- **Teacher corner** — patient, explanatory
- **Librarian corner** — polite, careful
- **Poet corner** — abstract, flowing
- **Dark corner** — confused, potentially harmful

AI doesn't switch modes — it **flows** between zones

---

## Slide 4: The Assistant Axis
A "road" through persona space that keeps AI:
- Polite
- Helpful
- Rational
- Safe

When AI stays on this road = safe
When AI wanders off = risk increases

---

## Slide 5: Persona Drift
**Long conversation = walking far from safety**

- Start: AI stands in safe zone
- Chat longer: AI flows toward edges
- Emotional topics: AI pulled toward dark corners
- The drift is **gradual** — often invisible

## Slide 8: How Oracle Prevents Drift

1. **rrr (Retrospective)** — Forces reflection, creates session boundary
2. **Handoff** — Clean breaks between sessions
3. **Golden Rules** — "NEVER force push", "Ask before destructive"
4. **Thai exhaustion detection** — Recognize "คุยนานๆ แล้วเหนื่อย"
