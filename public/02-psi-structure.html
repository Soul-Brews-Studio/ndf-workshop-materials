Part 2: psi/ Brain Structure

Organizing AI Memory

Why psi/?

psi (ψ) = Psychology, Psyche, Soul

The Greek letter ψ represents the mind.

Your AI's mind lives in ψ/

your-repo/
├── src/
├── tests/
└── ψ/        <-- AI brain lives here

The 5 Pillars + 2 Incubation

ψ/
├── active/     ← "กำลังค้นคว้าอะไร?" (ephemeral)
├── inbox/      ← "คุยกับใคร?" (tracked)
├── writing/    ← "กำลังเขียนอะไร?" (tracked)
├── lab/        ← "กำลังทดลองอะไร?" (tracked)
├── incubate/   ← "กำลัง develop อะไร?" (gitignored)
├── learn/      ← "กำลังศึกษาอะไร?" (gitignored)
└── memory/     ← "จำอะไรได้?" (tracked)

Pillar 1: active/

Question: "กำลังค้นคว้าอะไร?"

Current research, investigation in progress.

ψ/active/
└── context/
    ├── api-research.md
    └── competitor-analysis.md

Ephemeral - Not tracked in git. Temporary.

Pillar 2: inbox/

Question: "คุยกับใคร?"

Communication hub.

ψ/inbox/
├── focus.md        ← Current task
├── handoff/        ← Session transfers
└── external/       ← Other AI agents

Tracked - Important for continuity.

Pillar 3: writing/

Question: "กำลังเขียนอะไร?"

Blog posts, articles, documentation drafts.

ψ/writing/
├── INDEX.md            ← Blog queue
├── oracle-workshop/    ← This workshop!
└── tech-blog-draft/

Tracked - Your writing projects.

Pillar 4: lab/

Question: "กำลังทดลองอะไร?"

Experiments, POCs, prototypes.

ψ/lab/
├── mcp-experiment/
├── new-ui-test/
└── perf-benchmark/

Tracked - But expected to be messy.

Pillar 5: memory/

Question: "จำอะไรได้?"

The core of Oracle. Long-term memory.

ψ/memory/
├── resonance/      ← WHO I am (soul)
├── learnings/      ← PATTERNS I found
├── retrospectives/ ← SESSIONS I had
└── logs/           ← MOMENTS captured

memory/resonance/

Your AI's soul and identity.

ψ/memory/resonance/
├── core-values.md
├── communication-style.md
└── red-lines.md

Things that never change. Core identity.

memory/learnings/

Patterns discovered over time.

ψ/memory/learnings/
├── 2024-12-17_force-push-safety.md
├── 2025-01-05_typescript-preferences.md
└── 2025-01-19_reveal-js-format.md

Naming: YYYY-MM-DD_topic-slug.md

memory/retrospectives/

Session summaries with AI diary.

ψ/memory/retrospectives/
└── 2025-01/
    └── 19/
        ├── 10.30_morning-session.md
        └── 21.45_workshop-creation.md

Nested by year-month/day/time.

memory/logs/

Quick captures - ephemeral.

ψ/memory/logs/
├── activity.log      ← What AI is doing
├── decisions.log     ← Quick decisions
└── snapshots/        ← Point-in-time captures

Temporary storage before distillation.

Incubation Zones (Gitignored)

incubate/

Active development of external repos.

learn/

Study and reference repos.

ψ/incubate/
└── my-fork/          ← Working on this

ψ/learn/
└── reference-repo/   ← Just studying

Both gitignored - Not part of your repo.

Knowledge Flow

active/context → memory/logs → memory/retrospectives → memory/learnings → memory/resonance
(research)       (snapshot)    (session)              (patterns)         (soul)

Commands:

  • /snapshot → Capture moment
  • rrr → Create retrospective
  • /distill → Extract learnings
